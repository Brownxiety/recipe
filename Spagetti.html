Spagetti

Instruction

-spices

-crushed or diced tomatoes
-Italian sausage
-balsamic vinegar
-beef base
-spaghetti noodles, for serving

1. Cook 400g spaghetti in salted boiling water for 8-10 minutes until al dente; drain.
2. In a pan, saut√© 2 minced garlic cloves in 3 tbsp olive oil until fragrant, then add a can of crushed tomatoes, salt, and pepper; simmer for 15 minutes. Spread into a 12-inch circle and transfer to a preheated, floured pizza pan (or stone).
3. Toss spaghetti with the sauce, sprinkle with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese before serving.
