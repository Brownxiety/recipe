Instruction

1. Add the yeast to a small bowl with sugar and warm water (105-115°). Cover with saran wrap and let it sit for 5 minutes. In a medium bowl, add half of the flour, salt, and garlic powder. Stir to combine. Add the yeast mixture, oil, and gradually mix in remaining flour.
2. Knead for 3-5 minutes and transfer to a clean bowl. Cover with saran wrap and let rise until doubled by half, (30-60+ minutes), or overnight in the fridge. Gently punch it down and knead 5 times. Spread into a 12-inch circle and transfer to a preheated, floured pizza pan (or stone).
3. Top with cheese and toppings. Bake in a preheated 450° oven for 13-15 minutes. Remove, slice, and serve!
